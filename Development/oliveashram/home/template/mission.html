MISSION

The very name of our Ashram itself clearly states the mission of our Ashram Community. Missionaries of Sacred Mystery is a new missionary movement in the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church inspired by the Holy Qurbono to experience the Eucharistic presence of Christ Jesus. Olivians strongly believe in their monastic life, that their source of blessings and inspiration for their spirituality is none other than the Holy Qurbono. We begin this missionary movement with the hope to evangelize our nation through the Holy sacraments and preaching the Word of God in our words and action.

Mount Olive Ashram

Mount Olive Ashram is a Monastic Community for those who wish to live as a celibate community who can renounce this world and its pleasure to experience the love of God through the Holy Qurbono of the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church.

St. Joseph's Ashram Chapel

Missionaries of Sacred Mystery is a Celibate Missionary Movement in the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church. The source of their energy is the Holy Qurbono. Since the Patron Saint of this Ashram Community is St. Joseph the Righteous, The Chapel is known as St. Joseph's Chapel. We invite everyone to visit and pray in this Chapel.

Santhwanam Dhyana Mandiram

Santhwanam Dhyana Mandiram is a Retreat Centre where the faithful can come and stay for a week or more to meditate on the Holy Sacraments to have a personal encounter with our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.

Alvares Mor Yulius Mission Movement

Alvares Mor Yulius Mission Movement aims to send monks of our Ashram to various parts of our country to preach the word of God and to prepare the world to experience the Kingdom of God.

Mor Theophilos Palliative Care centre

The palliative care Centre will be in the name of the late Zachariah Mor Theophilos Thirumeni of Blessed Memory, the Former Visitor Bishop of Thadagam Christa Sishya Ashram. It was His Grace's long-cherished wish to take care of the bedridden patients. Ashram aims to help bedridden patients to feel comfortable and blessed before they leave this life.

Mor Philoxenos Old Age Home

Ashram aims to start an Old Age Home to take care the abandoned and lonely old people, especially from our Christian community who wish to have the Holy Sacraments regularly in their old age. We, the Olivians owe to the elderly to give them a peaceful and graceful old age. The Old age home is in the memory of Late Job Mor Philoxenos Metropolitan.

Mor Baselios Study Centre

Ashram aims to have a study centre where the young and old can come together to study Holy Bible and Holy Sacraments along with our Syriac language.
